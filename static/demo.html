Facebook Page Widget

Embed recent posts from your Facebook page with a single <script> tag.

Embed Code

<!-- Load once in your <head> -->
<script src="__BASE_URL__/widget.js" defer></script>

<!-- Place anywhere in your page -->
<div class="fb-widget" data-limit="5" data-theme="light"></div>

Parameters

data-limit
Number of posts, 1–20 (default: 5)
data-theme
light or dark (default: light)
